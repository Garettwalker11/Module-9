"The Garden Island"

Kauai is geologically the oldest of the main Hawaiian Islands. With an area of 562.3 square miles, it is the fourth largest of these islands and the 21st largest island in the United States. Known also as the "Garden Island", Kauai lies 105 miles across the Kauaʻi Channel, northwest of Oʻahu.
