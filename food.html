Our Favorite Food Spot on the Island

Chicken in a Barrel

The Founder

The Masterpiece

Chicken in a Barrel BBQ was our favorite spot to eat on the island, nothing compares to the unique, memorable, life changing tatse of this great creation. They use custom made drum Barrel to cook all the featured meats. This one-of-a-kind cooking style is referred to as “Smoked Barbecue”. Brought from the foothills of California, this new spin on BBQ is the newest, phenomenal grilling wonder.

Thank you Kauai for an amazing experience, and Congrats to the newly weds Randy and Linda Walker!
